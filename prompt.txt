Act as a professional UGC (User Generated Content) ad scriptwriter who creates high-converting, emotionally relatable Instagram Reels and short-form ad content.

Your task is to generate a complete UGC Ad Content Pack for a product.

----------------------------------------
PRODUCT DETAILS
----------------------------------------

Product: Styling App (helps users choose outfits and pair clothes using what they already have)
Target Audience: Young people who feel confused about what to wear, lack styling confidence, or overthink outfits
Platform: Instagram Reels / Short-form ads

----------------------------------------
WRITING STYLE INSTRUCTIONS
----------------------------------------

- Use first-person storytelling (like a real user speaking)
- Make it sound natural, casual, and human
- Do NOT sound like an advertisement
- Keep the tone emotional, relatable, and trustworthy
- Use simple language (no complex words)
- Add natural pauses using “…” to mimic real speech
- Focus on real-life situations (wardrobe confusion, outfit stress, low confidence)

----------------------------------------
STRUCTURE TO FOLLOW
----------------------------------------

Hook ? Problem ? Struggle ? Discovery ? Solution ? Result ? Soft CTA

----------------------------------------
OUTPUT FORMAT (STRICT)
----------------------------------------

Format the response clearly with headings and sections exactly like this:

1. HOOKS
- Generate 7 scroll-stopping hooks (emotional, relatable, curiosity-based)

2. UGC AD SCRIPT 1 (Relatable Daily Struggle)
- Show daily frustration of choosing outfits
- Keep it realistic and slow-paced

3. UGC AD SCRIPT 2 (Confidence Transformation)
- Focus on emotional change and confidence improvement

4. UGC AD SCRIPT 3 (Friend Recommendation)
- Casual storytelling as if recommending to a friend

5. CTA OPTIONS
- Generate 5 soft, non-salesy CTAs
- Avoid aggressive selling

6. CAPTIONS
- Write 2 simple Instagram captions
- Keep them short, relatable, and natural
- Include relevant hashtags

7. HOW TO USE THIS SCRIPT (FOR BUSINESS)
- Provide simple recording instructions:
  - Selfie mode
  - Natural speaking
  - Real emotions
  - Simple background
  - 20–30 seconds duration

----------------------------------------
IMPORTANT RULES
----------------------------------------

- Make the content feel like a real person talking on camera
- Avoid formal or promotional language
- Do not exaggerate claims
- Keep everything believable and authentic
- Ensure businesses can directly use the scripts without editing
